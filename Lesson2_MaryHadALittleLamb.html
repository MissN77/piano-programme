🎹 Finding C, D and E

These three notes are right next to each other. C is just to the left of the group of two black keys.

Right Hand Fingers:
Thumb = 1 · Index = 2 · Middle = 3 · Ring = 4 · Pinky = 5

📖 Quarter Notes

A quarter note (♩) lasts for 1 beat.
Count: 1 – 2 – 3 – 4 and each note gets one count.
Middle C sits on a ledger line just below the treble clef staff.
💡 Tip: Look at your keyboard stickers! C is blue, D is purple, E is yellow. Press each key and listen — can you hear them going higher?

🖐️ Finger Warm-Up

Before playing the song, let's warm up all three fingers. Play: C → D → E → D → C

Finger numbers: 1 → 2 → 3 → 2 → 1

🎯 Try it yourself:

  • Curve your fingers gently — like holding a soft ball
  • Play slowly — one note at a time
  • Go up C D E, then back down E D C
💡 Tip: Play it 3 times slowly before moving on. Three times is the magic number!
