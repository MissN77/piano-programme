What's Next? 🚀

Finishing this programme means you're ready to take the ABRSM Grade 1 exam — and ready to start thinking about what comes after that!

🎓 If you're taking the Grade 1 exam
You're prepared. You know the format, you've done two mock exams, and you know your pieces. Walk in, breathe, and enjoy it — the examiner genuinely wants you to do well.
📚 Grade 2 — what to look forward to
More pieces with more expressive range · Extra scales (F major, D major, B minor) · Slightly longer sight reading · Aural tests with more detail
Everything you've learnt here carries straight into Grade 2. You're already ahead.
🎵 Keep playing for fun
Learn songs you love · Explore YouTube tutorials · Try playing along to music you enjoy
The best pianists never stop playing just for the joy of it.
💡 Practise tip: Even 10 minutes a day keeps everything fresh. You've built the habit — keep it going!
💡 ASD tip:
If the idea of starting Grade 2 feels overwhelming, don't start yet. Spend a few weeks just playing pieces you love — things you already know and enjoy. Music should feel good. Come back to new challenges when you're ready.
