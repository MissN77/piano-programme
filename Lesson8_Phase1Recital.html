🤸 Warm Up (5 minutes)

Before every performance, musicians warm up their hands and mind. Follow these steps:

1
Shake hands loose — hang arms by your sides and shake gently for 10 seconds.
2
Scale walk — play C D E F G F E D C slowly, saying each note name.
3
Play your piece once slowly — ♩=60, no rushing.
4
Play it again at performance tempo — however fast feels comfortable.
5
Take 3 deep breaths — in for 4 counts, out for 4 counts.
💡 Feeling nervous? That is completely normal and okay — even professional concert pianists feel nervous. Nerves mean you care, which is a good thing.

🎤 Performance Time!

Ask your audience to sit down and be ready to listen. When you are ready:

1
Walk to the keyboard and sit up straight
2
Place your hands in position but do not play yet
3
Take one slow breath in and out
4
Play from start to finish — even if you make a mistake, keep going!
5
When you finish, take a bow 🎩
🎯 The golden rule: If you make a mistake, do not stop. Professional musicians keep going. The audience almost never notices a small mistake — they just hear the music.
