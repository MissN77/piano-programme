Mock Exam 1 🎓

This is your first full mock exam — run through everything exactly as you will on exam day. Same order, same timing, same focus. Let's see where you are!

🎯 Mock exam includes:
✅ All three pieces (List A, B and C) in order
✅ Scales — C, G and A minor, hands separately
✅ Sight reading — use the 30-second look-through
✅ Aural tests — pulse, dynamics, sing back
💡 Treat this exactly like the real exam. Sit down calmly, take a breath, and begin. Keep going through any mistakes — that's what examiners want to see!

Exam Order 📋

Follow this exact order. Use the timer to pace yourself — the real exam takes about 15 minutes:

1. Pieces — approx. 9 minutes
List A → List B → List C (or your preferred order)
Play each piece all the way through. Keep going if you make a mistake.
2. Scales — approx. 2 minutes
C major → G major → A minor harmonic
Hands separately for each. Steady tempo, smooth and even.
3. Sight Reading — approx. 1 minute
30-second look-through → play it once
Use your 5-question strategy: clef, time, key, rhythm, first note.
4. Aural Tests — approx. 3 minutes
Clap pulse → identify changes → sing back phrase
Listen carefully. Take your time before each answer.
