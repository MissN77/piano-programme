Exam Order 📋

Follow this exact order. Use the timer — aim to finish within 15 minutes:

1. Pieces — approx. 9 minutes
List A → List B → List C (or your preferred order)
Play each piece all the way through. Keep going if you make a mistake — no stopping!
2. Scales — approx. 2 minutes
C major → G major → A minor harmonic
Hands separately. Think of the sharps before you start each one.
3. Sight Reading — approx. 1 minute
30-second look-through → play it once
Clef · time · key · rhythm · first note. Five questions in 30 seconds.
4. Aural Tests — approx. 3 minutes
Clap pulse → identify changes → sing back phrase
Take a breath before each test. You've practised this — trust yourself.

🎯 Final exam preparation:
1. Do a calm, full run-through the day before — not to drill, just to feel at home
2. Sleep well the night before — your brain consolidates learning during sleep
3. Eat breakfast on exam day — your concentration depends on it
4. Arrive early, find a quiet spot, breathe slowly before you go in
5. Remember: the examiner wants you to do well. They're on your side!
