📖 The Grand Staff

Music is written on lines called a staff. Piano music uses two staffs joined together — the Grand Staff.

𝄞
Treble Clef

Right hand (RH)
Higher notes

𝄢
Bass Clef

Left hand (LH)
Lower notes

Remember the treble clef lines (bottom to top):
E – G – B – D – F
👉 "Every Good Boy Deserves Food"
Remember the treble clef spaces (bottom to top):
F – A – C – E
👉 They spell FACE! 😊
💡 You don't need to read sheet music perfectly yet — just start to recognise the shapes. It takes time!
