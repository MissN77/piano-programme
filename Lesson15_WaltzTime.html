Waltz Time 💃

What is 3/4 Time?

In 3/4 time, we count 3 beats per bar.

1
STRONG
da
2
soft
da
3
soft
da

Say it aloud: "ONE two three, ONE two three…"

🕺 The Waltz Feel
It swings like a rocking boat. Beat 1 is strong, beats 2 and 3 are gentle.
"Oom-pah-pah, Oom-pah-pah"

The Time Signature

The 3 on top means: 3 beats per bar
The 4 on bottom means: a quarter note (♩) gets one beat

3
4

💡 Remember: 4/4 has 4 beats (march). 3/4 has 3 beats (waltz)!

Happy Birthday 🎂

One of the most famous waltz tunes ever!

Count as you play: 1-2-3, 1-2-3…
Beat 1 is always the strongest!
Tap any note to hear it. Use Play to hear the full melody.

Practice Tips 💡

🎯 This Week's Focus
Feel the waltz swing. Count 1-2-3 out loud every time!
Step-by-step practice:
1️⃣ Count "1-2-3" aloud with a clap on beat 1
2️⃣ Play just the melody slowly
3️⃣ Count AND play at the same time
4️⃣ Speed up when it feels comfortable
✅ You're ready to move on when:
• You can count to 3 in time
• Happy Birthday sounds like a song, not just notes!
